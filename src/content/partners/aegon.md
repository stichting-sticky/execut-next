## Build your own future at Aegon

Whether you're just graduated from college or in your first job: Aegon is always looking for talent. With 26,000 employees worldwide, we ensure that customers can make conscious choices for their financial future. We are an insurer, pension company and asset manager in one.

Aegon is maybe not known for its IT, but behind the scenes Aegon has a large IT-backbone using several modern technologies (AWS/Azure, robotics, ML, datalake, CI/CD, Azure Dev-ops, etc), which run large parts of our business. Developing algorithms for machine learning, building cloud-native applications and working on a unique data lake... These are just a few of the things our IT professionals are working on. Each of them talented professionals that are leading the way in IT. Through IT they are making a world of difference for our customers. Will you join us?
