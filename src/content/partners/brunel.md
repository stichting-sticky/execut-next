## Build a rich resume in no time

You will have plenty of choice at the beginning of your career. But what is the right choice? Do you choose a company that seems the most fun, is located closest to home or the one that is offering the best opportunities to develop yourself? Difficult? Not at all, choose Brunel and you choose all of them. You will start a job that matches your qualities and you will receive a proper fixed salary. You will work on challenging assignments at different top companies within your field of expertise.

## Personal development

Your career at Brunel is all about growth. If you need some good advice, Brunel is ready to help you out. Together with your consultant and our client, we will create a personal development plan for you. Based on this plan Brunel offers the opportunity to develop your professional knowledge as well as your soft skills. By developing both aspects, you will become an experienced professional.

## Secondment

Brunel offers many different types of contract but in most cases, our employees are seconded out to our clients. This means you will work on various projects at interesting companies. Because of the temporary nature of project-based employment you will enjoy a high level of variety in your work while having the certainty of a fixed contract.
