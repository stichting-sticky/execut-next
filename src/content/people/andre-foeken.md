André Foeken is the CTO of Nedap Healthcare. Nedap is a multinational with over nine hundred employees and is active across several different markets. Nedap Healthcare focuses mostly on the local Dutch market and supplies the Dutch care market with software solutions. Besides developing applications, Andre is interested in shaping organisations and communication strategies.
