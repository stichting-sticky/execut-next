Tom is a Forensic, Cybercrime and IT security expert, working for the PwC Forensic Technology Solutions practice in The Netherlands.

Prior to joining PwC in 2013, he used to work for EY and Fox-IT where he was, in the role of Operations Manager, responsible for large IT incident response investigations and management of the Forensic IT team.

Tom is interested in IT Security, forensics and ISC2 certified. he was employed for one year at a large company in The Hague in the role of Corporate Information Security Officer during his time working for Fox-IT.
