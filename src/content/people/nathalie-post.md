Nathalie Post is cofounder at DEUS: human(ity)-centered AI. As Head of Strategy, she helps organisations apply artificial intelligence responsibly to create value for people, business & society. She also hosts The Human-Centered AI Podcast, featuring inspiring stories of people who are shaping the future of artificial intelligence in a human(ity) & planet-centered way. Previously she worked with global enterprises, corporate ventures and startups at Accenture Interactive as Innovation Program Lead, and holds a degree in Artificial Intelligence.
