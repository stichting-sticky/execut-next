---
title: Making money with software
speaker: andre-foeken
---

There are many ways to profit of the things you build. In this talk I will go into several common ways to monetise your software, ranging from asking money for your side projects, selling your time, and even some strategies for big companies. It all sounds easier than it actually is, in this talk I will try to give some tips and tricks specifically geared towards developers wanting to make an extra buck.
