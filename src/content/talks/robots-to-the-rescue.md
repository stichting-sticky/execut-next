---
title: Robots to the Rescue
speaker: arnoud-visser
---

Arnoud Visser will give a presentation about his work in robotics and the RoboCup Rescue Simulation League. In this league a disaster is simulated and a group of robots has to work together to find victims. Arnoud will sketch a picture of his work within this league, what the current challenges are and how they can be solved. Finally, he will tell you what the future of his field will look like.
