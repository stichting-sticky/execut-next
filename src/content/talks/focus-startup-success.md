---
title: 'Focus: How can you increase the chances of a startup being successful?'
speaker: patrick-lee
---

Why do startups often fail? How can you increase the chances of a startup being successful?

In this talk, Patrick discusses why focus is the most important thing for a startup to have. He discusses his own experience with startups, mistakes that everybody makes and why startups often fail. He then discusses how to prevent these mistakes and how to increase the chance of a startup being successful using focus. After the talk there will be a fireside chat in which Patrick will answer questions about startups and his own experience.
